Our Story

From a shared dream to your neighborhood café

How It All Began

Citrus & Ember started in 2019 when lifelong friends Maya Chen and David Okonkwo decided to combine their passions. Maya brought years of experience as a specialty coffee roaster, while David contributed his love for baking and community building. Together, they envisioned a space where exceptional coffee meets genuine hospitality.

The name reflects our philosophy: the bright, zesty energy of citrus paired with the warm, comforting glow of ember. We wanted every visit to feel both refreshing and welcoming, like catching up with an old friend over a perfect cup of coffee.

Our Mission

We believe great coffee should be accessible to everyone, crafted with intention, and served in a space that feels like home. Our mission is simple: create moments of joy, one cup at a time. We're committed to sustainable sourcing, supporting local suppliers, and fostering real connections in our community.

What We Stand For

Quality First

We source our beans directly from farmers who share our commitment to excellence. Every batch is roasted locally in small quantities to ensure peak freshness and flavor in every cup we serve.

Community Focus

We host monthly open mic nights, display work from local artists, and partner with neighborhood organizations. This café belongs to Willow Creek, and we're proud to give back to the community that supports us.

Sustainability

From compostable cups to our zero-waste baking initiative, we're constantly working to reduce our environmental footprint. Small changes add up to big impact when we all work together.

Come Say Hello

We'd love to share a cup with you and hear your story too. Stop by anytime—there's always a seat waiting for you.
