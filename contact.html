Common Questions

Do you offer catering?
Yes! We provide coffee service and pastry platters for events of all sizes. Contact us at least 48 hours in advance for catering orders, and we'll work with you to create the perfect spread.
Is there WiFi available?
Absolutely. Free high-speed WiFi is available for all customers. Just ask any staff member for the password when you order.
Can I book the café for a private event?
We offer private bookings for groups of 20 or more on Sunday evenings. Fill out the contact form above or call us to discuss your event.
