Welcome to Citrus & Ember

Where bright citrus flavors meet the warmth of freshly roasted coffee. We craft every cup with care, source our beans from sustainable farms, and bake our pastries fresh each morning. Join us for an experience that ignites your day.

What Makes Us Special

Artisan Coffee

Small-batch roasted beans sourced directly from farmers in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala. Every cup tells a story of quality and craft.

Fresh Pastries

Baked in-house daily using locally sourced ingredients. From flaky croissants to our signature citrus scones, there's always something delicious waiting for you.

Cozy Atmosphere

Warm lighting, comfortable seating, and the gentle hum of good conversation. Our space is designed for connection, creativity, and comfort.
